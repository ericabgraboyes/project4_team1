"Goodbye, So Soon" is a song from Disney's 1986 animated classic film The Great Mouse Detective.
It is sung by Professor Ratigan and his thugs in a track Ratigan recorded for when he finally gets the better of his archenemy Basil. The song is co-written by Henry Mancini, Larry Grossman & Ellen Fitzhugh.
